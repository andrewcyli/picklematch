Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

PickleballMatch.Fun is designed to work with minimal data collection. When you create a game session, the information you enter (player names, scores, match results) is stored temporarily to power the live leaderboard and round robin functionality. This data is not associated with your personal identity unless you voluntarily provide contact information through the feedback form.

Cookies

We use essential cookies to maintain your session while the app is in use. We also use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site — this collects anonymous usage data such as pages visited and browser type. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics.

Advertisements on this site are served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to personalize ads based on your preferences. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

How We Use Your Information

Game session data (player names, scores, match results) is used solely to power the round robin organizer and live leaderboard features. Feedback submissions are used to improve the product. We do not sell or share personally identifiable information with third parties.

Data Retention

Game session data is stored temporarily. Feedback submissions are retained until you request deletion. Contact us at the address below to request removal of any submitted data.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage tracking
  • Google AdSense — advertisement delivery, may use cookies
  • Supabase — database hosting for game sessions and feedback

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: hello@pickleballmatch.fun
